Help & Support

If you need assistance, please refer to the resources below. For technical issues, please contact your administrator.

How to Use This Application

This application is designed to help you track and manage access to sensitive datasets. As a user, you can view the status of your data access requests and see a history of your approved access logs.

Administrators have additional privileges, including managing user accounts, approving or denying access requests, and auditing access history.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues, try these steps:

  • Ensure you are logged in with the correct account.
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
  • Contact your system administrator if the issue persists.
