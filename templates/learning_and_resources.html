Getting Started

Welcome to the Data Access Tracker! This platform is designed to help you and your team keep a centralized, auditable log of all data access events. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Log In: Use your provided credentials to log in to the system.
  2. Navigate to your Dashboard: As a user, your dashboard is your home base. Here you can see your profile and recent activity.
  3. Log Data Access: Click the " Log Access" button on your dashboard to record a new data access event. Fill out the form with details like the dataset name, purpose, and date.
  4. View Your History: All your logged access events will be visible on your dashboard and a dedicated "My Logs" page.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Data Access Tracker is a centralized system for logging and monitoring data access, ensuring a clear and compliant record of who accessed what data, and for what purpose.

Your individual access logs are visible to you on your dashboard. Administrators have access to a complete history of all logs across the organization for auditing purposes.

For now, dataset access requests are not handled within this system. You should follow your company's standard procedure for requesting new data access.
